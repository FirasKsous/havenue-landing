Marketing Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Applies to: havenue.co and related Havenue services

1. Who this policy covers

This policy explains how Havenue LLC handles optional marketing communications. It applies to anyone who has submitted their email address through the Havenue website and chosen to receive product updates or industry content from us.

2. Marketing communications are optional and opt-in only

Havenue only sends marketing emails to people who explicitly opt in. Every marketing consent checkbox on the Havenue website is unchecked by default. We never assume consent from a signup, a demo request, or a free-trial activation.

When you opt in, you are agreeing to receive occasional emails from Havenue that may include:

  • Product updates and new feature announcements
  • Hospitality industry insights and operational guides
  • Invitations to webinars, events, or early-access programs
  • Relevant resources such as calculators, templates, or benchmarks

3. Where consent is collected

Marketing consent is currently collected in one place on the Havenue website: the exit-intent modal that offers the 2026 Hospitality Profit Calculator. That modal contains an optional, unchecked checkbox with the label "Email me occasional Havenue product updates and hospitality insights. This is optional and off by default."

Submitting your email to request a demo or to start a free trial does not enable marketing communications. Those flows collect your email for service and transactional purposes only.

4. Transactional emails are separate

Some emails from Havenue are not marketing. Transactional and service emails include:

  • Trial confirmation and account setup emails
  • Billing receipts and subscription change notifications
  • Direct replies from the Havenue team to demo or sales requests you submit

These emails are sent because they are necessary to deliver the service you requested. They are not conditional on marketing consent and cannot be opted out of while your account remains active.

5. How to unsubscribe

You can withdraw your marketing consent at any time. Every marketing email from Havenue includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom of the message. Clicking that link removes you from all optional marketing communications immediately.

You can also unsubscribe by emailing us directly at contact@havenue.co. We will process your request within five business days.

Unsubscribing from marketing emails does not affect your account, your trial, or any transactional communications related to your subscription.

6. What we do not do

Havenue does not currently send SMS, WhatsApp, or push notification marketing. We do not run paid retargeting campaigns using your personal email address. We do not sell or share your email address with third-party advertisers.
