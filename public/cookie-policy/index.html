2. What cookies and similar technologies are

A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your browser or device when you visit. Cookies let a site remember information between page loads and across sessions. We also use localStorage and sessionStorage, which are browser storage mechanisms that work similarly to cookies but are not transmitted with every HTTP request. This policy covers all of these technologies collectively.

3. How we collect your consent

When you first visit the Havenue website, a consent banner appears at the bottom of the screen. Non-essential cookies and tracking are off by default. You can choose to accept or decline analytics cookies using the banner controls. Your choice is saved in your browser so you are not asked again on subsequent visits.

You can withdraw or change your consent at any time by clearing your browser’s cookies and local storage for this site, which will reset your preferences and show the consent banner again on your next visit.

4. Cookie categories

We use two categories of cookies on this site.

4.1 Essential cookies

Essential cookies are required for the site to function. They do not track you for advertising or analytics purposes. We place these without asking for consent because they are strictly necessary to deliver the service you have requested.

Name Type Purpose Duration
havenue_consent localStorage Stores your cookie consent preferences (analytics: true/false, marketing: true/false) so the banner is not shown on every visit. Persistent (until cleared)

4.2 Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. These are only placed if you accept analytics cookies via the consent banner. If you decline, none of the following are set.

Name Type Purpose Duration
havenue_aid First-party cookie A randomly generated anonymous identifier that links behavioral events (page views, CTA clicks, scroll depth) to a single browser session. This ID is used to build a lead score and is associated with your email address only if you voluntarily submit a contact form. 365 days
havenue_utm sessionStorage Stores UTM campaign parameters (source, medium, campaign, term, content) from the URL you arrived on. Used to attribute visits to marketing campaigns. Cleared when the browser tab or window is closed. Session
Google Analytics 4 (_ga, _ga_*) Third-party cookie Google Analytics 4 is loaded dynamically only after you accept analytics cookies. It measures aggregate site traffic and user behavior. Google may process data on servers outside the UAE. For details, see Google’s Privacy Policy. Up to 2 years (Google-managed)

Behavioral events collected under analytics consent (such as section views, CTA clicks, and scroll depth) are sent to a Havenue-operated database to calculate a lead score. This score helps our team prioritize follow-up with visitors who have shown strong interest in the product.

5. Marketing emails and marketing consent

We treat consent for marketing emails separately from analytics cookies. Marketing consent covers receiving product updates, promotional offers, and other commercial communications from Havenue by email.

Marketing consent is off by default and is only collected when you explicitly opt in, for example by ticking a marketing opt-in checkbox on a contact or trial signup form. Accepting analytics cookies via the cookie banner does not constitute consent to receive marketing emails.

The consent banner stores your analytics preference in the havenue_consent localStorage entry described above. Optional marketing-email opt-ins are collected separately in the relevant lead-capture form flow and are not switched on by the cookie banner.

6. Third-party services in scope

The only third-party analytics service currently active on this site is Google Analytics 4, which is loaded only after analytics consent is granted (see section 4.2 above). We do not use advertising networks, retargeting pixels, or social media tracking scripts on this site.

7. Your rights and choices

  • Withdraw consent: Clear cookies and local storage for this site in your browser settings. The consent banner will reappear on your next visit.
  • Browser controls: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality.
  • Opt out of Google Analytics: You can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent data being sent to Google Analytics across all sites.

We may update this Cookie Policy when we add new features, change our analytics setup, or when regulations require it. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated via the consent banner.
